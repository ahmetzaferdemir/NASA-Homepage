Explore The World

Earth, known as the third planet of our solar system, is the only known place where life exists. Formed approximately 4.5 billion years ago, this magnificent planet has unique characteristics in terms of water, atmosphere and diversity.

Quite a Masterpiece

About 70% of the Earth's surface is covered with water, allowing various seas, oceans and lakes to form. It is also home to a variety of natural beauties such as impressive mountain ranges, fertile agricultural land, extensive meadows and dense forests.

We Are Not Alone In This World

However, our world also faces environmental problems. Problems such as climate change, depletion of natural resources and loss of biodiversity pose serious threats to the sustainability of our planet. It is therefore of paramount importance that the earth be protected and managed sustainably.
